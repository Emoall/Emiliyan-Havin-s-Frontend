Witwatia

Witwatia (from the Egyptian Arabic Wit Wat meaning "large, flapping wings") is an extinct genus of giant bat that lived in the Al Fayyum in Egypt, during the late Eocene some years ago. It is known from a lower jaw and teeth. Two species have been named: the type species W. schlosseri, and W. eremicus. This is the only information known about this huge bats.
